Industrial Craft: The Rubber Soul

A technical analysis of the intersection between Brazilian artisanal leatherwork and the brutalist necessity of the urban sprawl.

// THE NARRATIVE

Cavalera’s "Industrial Craft" series is a manifesto written in recycled rubber and heavy-gauge nylon. It honors the sertão artisan—whose hands transform raw materials—while engineering for the high-friction environment of the concrete jungle. Each piece is a technical rebellion against planned obsolescence.
